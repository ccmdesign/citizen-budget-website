About

Interactive budget simulator used by over 50 cities; reaching 6 million citizens

Citizen Budget is a product of Open North - a non-profit organization dedicated to increasing government transparency on one hand and citizen engagement in the democratic process, on the other. Used successfully by many municipalities and school districts, Citizen Budget is a powerful and innovative tool that helps build community relations, educates residents and gauges residents’ priorities on operating or capital budgets.

Unlike a regular survey, Citizen Budget simulator shows the financial impacts in real-time, educating residents about the tradeoffs and constraints faced by their municipality. Unlike budget visualization software, Citizen Budget is an interactive platform that provides a two-way dialogue between you and your community, providing both parties with rich and detailed information. Your residents can conveniently access your Citizen Budget consultation anytime and at their own pace, making it also a clear improvement over public meetings which involve travel, time, childminding and other accessibility constraints.
