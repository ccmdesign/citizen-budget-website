Newmarket

Ontario

The Town of Newmarket invited its residents to participate in an online consultation on its annual operating budget with the goal of consulting residents on how they would like to see their tax dollars invested in Town services.

Prior to implementing the on-line tool, Newmarket conducted paper-based surveys at Town events and various locations including our regional mall. After engaging the services of Open North to prepare a web-based version, participation in the budget survey tripled.

- Cindy Wackett, Corporate Project Consultant, Town of Newmarket
